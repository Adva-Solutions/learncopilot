A NOTE FROM ERAN CHEN

As we enter spring 2025, ODA continues to push the boundaries of what architecture can achieve for communities and cities. This quarter marks several milestones -- from the completion of our Wynwood Arts Mixed-Use project in Miami to exciting new commissions that will shape skylines from New York to Dubai.

Our work has always been driven by a simple conviction: great buildings should give more to their neighborhoods than they take. PROJECT SPOTLIGHT: WYNWOOD ARTS MIXED-USE

IMAGE: [wynwood-ground-floor-gallery.jpg]
IMAGE: [wynwood-courtyard-aerial.jpg]
IMAGE: [wynwood-facade-detail.jpg]

Our Wynwood Arts Mixed-Use building is now complete and fully occupied. This 295,000 SF development in Miami's creative heart combines 180 residential units with 35,000 SF of ground-floor gallery and retail space -- all designed with ODA's signature porosity concept that dissolves the boundary between building and street.

The project achieved LEED Platinum certification with a 42% energy reduction and over 60% recycled content in its structural concrete. Every ground-floor commercial space is occupied by local artists and independent businesses.

Key Facts:
- 295,000 SF | 180 units | 16 floors
- LEED Platinum Certified
- 42% energy reduction vs. baseline
- 100% ground-floor occupancy by local businesses

IN PROGRESS: SHORE CLUB RESIDENCES

IMAGE: [shore-club-rendering-southeast.jpg]

Construction documents are underway for the Shore Club Residences, a luxury condominium tower in Fort Lauderdale. The 22-story building features a hybrid facade combining precast concrete at the base with terracotta rain screen panels on the residential tower -- a design that balances durability, warmth, and cost efficiency.

The project is on track for Building Department submission in May 2025, with construction expected to begin in late 2025.

Location: Fort Lauderdale, FL
Size: 385,000 SF | Residential Condo
Status: Construction Documents

NEW COMMISSION: THE MIRA HOTEL & RESIDENCES

IMAGE: [mira-hotel-concept-rendering.jpg]

ODA has been selected to design The Mira Hotel & Residences in Dubai Marina -- our first project in the Middle East. This mixed-use tower will combine a luxury hotel with branded residences, featuring terraced gardens on the hotel floors and a signature rooftop restaurant with panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf.

We're currently in Schematic Design and will present the concept to the client's design committee in April.

Location: Dubai, UAE
Size: 410,000 SF | Hospitality + Residential
Status: Schematic Design
