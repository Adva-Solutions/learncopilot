EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Q1 2025 was a strong quarter for ODA. Revenue increased 8% year-over-year, driven by active CD phases on Shore Club and Mayflower, a new international commission (The Mira Hotel in Dubai), and continued CA work on Harbor View and Wynwood. Utilization rates averaged 77% across the firm, slightly below our 80% target due to the ramp-up period on new commissions. Staffing remains tight, with two new hires scheduled for April and two active searches ongoing.

Key highlights:
- Revenue: $1,435,000 (vs. $1,330,000 Q1 2024, +8%)
- New commissions: 2 (Jersey City Waterfront Tower, The Mira Hotel & Residences)
- Active projects: 10
- Staff: 26 full-time (28 budgeted)
