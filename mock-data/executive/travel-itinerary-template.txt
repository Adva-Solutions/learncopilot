IMPORTANT NOTES
===============================================================================

- Dubai time zone: GST (UTC+4), 8 hours ahead of EST
- Currency: UAE Dirham (AED). 1 USD ≈ 3.67 AED
- Credit cards widely accepted; carry ~AED 500 cash for taxis and tips
- Weather in April: Hot, 30-35°C (86-95°F), humid. Sunscreen essential.
- Dress code: Business attire for meetings, smart casual for dinners
- Ramadan: Check dates (may affect restaurant hours and business customs)
- Tipping: 10-15% at restaurants (often included as service charge)
- Electrical: Type G plugs (UK-style). Bring adapter.
- SIM/Data: Hotel WiFi + international roaming on AT&T plan should suffice
