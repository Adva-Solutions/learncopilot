KEY TAKEAWAY FOR ODA PROJECTS
=============================================================================

When working on projects in both NYC and Florida:

1. WIND: Florida projects (especially HVHZ) require significantly more facade 
   documentation. Start collecting NOAs early -- they can take 3-6 weeks to obtain.

2. STAIRS: NYC stairs are more restrictive (7" risers vs 7-3/4"). Always design to 
   NYC standards if the project may be replicated or compared across offices.

3. ENERGY: NYC has carbon emissions penalties (LL97) that don't exist in Florida. 
   NYC projects need energy modeling early in SD to verify compliance.

4. FACADE: NYC requires FISP-compliant facade design. Florida requires hurricane 
   impact testing. Both add cost and documentation but for different reasons.

5. PERMITS: NYC allows professional certification (faster) but FL process is 
   generally shorter overall. Factor HVHZ documentation into FL permit timelines.

6. PARKING: NYC has eliminated parking minimums in Manhattan. Florida jurisdictions 
   still require significant parking, affecting podium design and project cost.
