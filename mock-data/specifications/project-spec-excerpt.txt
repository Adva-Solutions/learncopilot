SECTION 07 42 13 - TERRACOTTA RAINSCREEN PANELS

PART 1 - GENERAL

1.01 SUMMARY
A. Section includes exterior terracotta rainscreen cladding system for
   residential tower floors 8 through 22.
B. Related sections:
   - Section 07 21 00: Thermal Insulation
   - Section 07 62 00: Sheet Metal Flashing
   - Section 07 92 00: Joint Sealants
   - Section 05 40 00: Cold-Formed Metal Framing (substructure)

1.02 QUALITY ASSURANCE
A. Manufacturer: Minimum 20 years experience producing terracotta
   rainscreen systems for exterior building applications.
B. Installer: Minimum 5 completed projects of similar scope and
   complexity within the last 7 years. HVHZ experience required.
C. Design wind load: 185 mph ultimate wind speed per ASCE 7-16
   (High-Velocity Hurricane Zone).
D. Impact resistance: Large missile impact per TAS 201-94.
E. Florida Product Approval or Miami-Dade County NOA required.

1.03 ACCEPTABLE MANUFACTURERS
A. Moeding Alphaton (basis of design)
B. NBK Architectural Terracotta
C. Boston Valley Terra Cotta
D. Substitution requests must be submitted in writing with full
   technical data, color samples, and HVHZ compliance documentation
   at least 30 days prior to submittal deadline.

1.04 SUBMITTALS
A. Product data: Complete catalog cuts, technical specifications,
   and installation instructions.
B. Shop drawings: Panel layout, dimensions, joint widths, corner
   conditions, and interface details with adjacent materials.
C. Samples: 12"x12" panel samples in each specified color (minimum
   3 samples per color for color range approval).
D. Test reports: Wind load testing, impact testing, water penetration
   testing per ASTM E331, fire testing per ASTM E84.
E. Florida Product Approval or Miami-Dade County NOA.
F. Warranty documentation.

PART 2 - PRODUCTS

2.01 TERRACOTTA PANELS
A. Material: Extruded natural clay terracotta, kiln-fired.
B. Finish: Natural matte, unglazed.
C. Color: Warm Sienna (Moeding color ref: S-482) for primary field.
   Sand Beige (Moeding color ref: S-310) for accent bands at every
   third floor.
D. Panel dimensions: 300mm x 1200mm nominal (11.8" x 47.2").
E. Panel thickness: 30mm (1.18").
F. Weight: Maximum 45 kg/m2 (9.2 psf) installed.
G. Water absorption: Maximum 6% per ASTM C373.
H. Freeze-thaw resistance: Minimum 50 cycles per ASTM C1026 (N/A
   for Florida but required for product qualification).
I. Flame spread index: 0 (non-combustible per ASTM E84).
J. Smoke developed index: 0.

2.02 SUBSTRUCTURE
A. Vertical rails: Aluminum T-6 alloy, 6063-T6, minimum 3mm thickness.
B. Horizontal rails: Aluminum, 6063-T6, minimum 2.5mm thickness.
C. Brackets: Stainless steel 316, thermally broken.
D. Fasteners: Stainless steel 304, torque-controlled.
E. All aluminum components: Anodized or powder-coated to match panel color.

2.03 CAVITY
A. Minimum cavity depth: 6 inches (150mm) from face of insulation
   to back of terracotta panel.
B. Ventilation: Open joints at top and bottom of each floor for
   continuous airflow. Minimum 3/8" open joint between panels.
C. Fire stops: Non-combustible mineral wool cavity barriers at every
   floor line and at maximum 20-foot intervals horizontally.
D. Drainage: Cavity must drain to weep holes at each floor line.

PART 3 - EXECUTION

3.01 INSTALLATION
A. Install in accordance with manufacturer's written instructions.
B. Verify substrate alignment before beginning installation.
   Maximum substrate deviation: 1/4" in 10 feet.
C. Install thermal insulation and air/vapor barrier before substructure.
D. Install cavity fire stops at each floor line before panels.
E. Panel joint width: 3/8" nominal (8mm). Open joint, no sealant.
F. Corner panels: Pre-fabricated mitered corners by manufacturer.
   Field-cut corners not permitted.
G. Protect installed panels from construction damage with removable
   coverings during adjacent work.

SECTION 08 71 00 - DOOR HARDWARE

PART 1 - GENERAL

1.01 SUMMARY
A. Section includes all door hardware for the project including:
   - Residential unit entry doors
   - Common area doors
   - Stairwell doors
   - Mechanical room doors
   - Parking garage doors

1.02 QUALITY ASSURANCE
A. All hardware shall be from a single manufacturer or coordinated
   group to ensure keying system compatibility.
B. Hardware shall comply with BHMA/ANSI standards.
C. Fire-rated hardware shall be UL listed for the required rating.
D. ADA-compliant hardware on all doors on accessible routes.

1.03 ACCEPTABLE MANUFACTURERS
A. Schlage (basis of design for locksets)
B. LCN (basis of design for closers)
C. Hager (basis of design for hinges)
D. Substitutions: Equal products by ASSA ABLOY, Allegion, or
   dormakaba are acceptable with prior approval.

B. Product data for each hardware item.
C. Keying schedule and master key system diagram.
D. Electrified hardware wiring diagrams.
E. Fire-rated assembly documentation (UL listing).
F. Warranty documentation.
