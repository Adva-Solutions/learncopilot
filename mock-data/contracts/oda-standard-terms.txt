This document outlines ODA's standard positions on key contract terms.
Use this as a reference when reviewing owner-architect agreements and
negotiating contract modifications.

===============================================================================
FEE & PAYMENT
===============================================================================

Fee Methods (acceptable):
  [OK] Time & Resources with not-to-exceed cap
  [OK] Percentage of Construction Cost
  [OK] Fixed/Lump Sum
  [NO] Contingency-based or success-based fees

Fee Ranges by Typology:
  Residential Condo:     5.0% - 7.0% of construction cost
  Residential Rental:    4.5% - 6.5%
  Hospitality:           5.5% - 8.0%
  Mixed-Use:             5.0% - 7.5%
  Cultural:              6.0% - 9.0%
  International (add):   +1.0% - 2.0% premium

Phase Distribution (standard):
  SD: 15%  |  DD: 25%  |  CD: 30%  |  Bid/Neg: 10%  |  CA: 20%

  Acceptable range:
  SD: 12-20%  |  DD: 20-28%  |  CD: 25-35%  |  Bid: 5-12%  |  CA: 15-25%

  RED FLAG: Any distribution that front-loads less than 35% in SD+DD phases

Payment Terms:
  Standard: Net 30 days
  Acceptable: Net 45 days
  RED FLAG: Net 60+ days
  Late payment interest: 1.0% - 1.5% per month (insist on inclusion)

===============================================================================
REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
===============================================================================

Standard Cap: 10-12% of total fee (domestic projects)
International Projects: 13-15% of total fee
  RED FLAG: Caps below 10% -- we consistently run 10-12% on reimbursables

Items that MUST be reimbursable:
  - Printing and reproduction
  - Permit and filing fees
  - Travel (if project >50 miles from office)
  - Models and presentation materials
  - Additional insurance premiums required by Owner/lender
  - Shipping costs

Markup on reimbursables: 10% administrative handling (standard)

===============================================================================
INSURANCE
===============================================================================

Professional Liability (E&O):
  ODA Current Policy: $3,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate
  
  Acceptable requests:
  [OK] $3M / $5M (our standard)
  [OK] $5M / $10M (if client pays additional premium as reimbursable)
  [NO] $10M+ without discussion -- premium becomes significant

  Tail coverage:
  [OK] 3 years post-completion (standard in our policy)
  [OK] 5 years (acceptable for large projects)
  [NO] 10+ years without significant premium negotiation

General Liability:
  ODA Current Policy: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $4,000,000 aggregate
  
  [OK] Up to $5M aggregate if requested
  [NO] Requests exceeding our policy limits without Owner funding

KEY PRINCIPLE: If the Owner or lender requires insurance beyond our
standard policy limits, the additional premium is a reimbursable expense.
This is non-negotiable.

===============================================================================
LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
===============================================================================

Standard Position: Liability capped at 1x total fees paid
  [OK] 1x total fees (our standard)
  [OK] 1.5x total fees (acceptable for large/high-profile projects)
  [NEGOTIATE] 2x total fees -- counter with tiered approach (see below)
  [NO] Unlimited liability
  [NO] Liability tied to construction cost or project value

Tiered Approach (use when client pushes above 1.5x):
  General professional liability claims: 1.5x total fees
  Willful misconduct / gross negligence: 2x total fees
  
  This has been accepted on:
  - Shore Club Residences (2024)
  - Brickell Hospitality Tower (2022)

Consequential Damages:
  [ALWAYS] Mutual waiver of consequential damages
  [NO] Accept liability for lost profits, lost revenue, or delay damages

===============================================================================
TERMINATION
===============================================================================

Cure Period:
  Standard: 30 days
  Acceptable: 20-30 days
  RED FLAG: Less than 20 days -- insufficient to cure most breaches

Termination Fee:
  Standard: 5% of remaining unearned fee
  International: 10% of remaining unearned fee (covers non-recoverable costs)
  [NO] No termination fee -- we need protection for staff commitments

Termination for Convenience:
  Standard: 30 days' written notice (domestic)
  International: 60 days' written notice (due to travel and coordination)

Upon Termination, Owner must pay:
  [ALWAYS] All services performed to date
  [ALWAYS] All reimbursable expenses incurred
  [ALWAYS] Termination fee
  [INTERNATIONAL] Non-refundable travel expenses already booked

===============================================================================
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
===============================================================================

Standard: Instruments of Service remain ODA property
License: Owner gets a license to use for THIS project only
License Condition: Contingent on full payment of all fees

  [NO] Transfer of ownership of design documents to Owner
  [NO] License to use design on OTHER projects without separate agreement
  [OK] License to use for facility management and renovation of completed project

===============================================================================
SCHEDULE & SCOPE CHANGES
===============================================================================

Owner Review Periods:
  Standard: 14 calendar days at each milestone
  Acceptable: 7-21 calendar days
  RED FLAG: Less than 7 days or no defined review period

Scope Changes:
  [ALWAYS] Changes after DD require written authorization + fee adjustment
  [ALWAYS] Code changes enacted after agreement date are Additional Services
  [ALWAYS] Extended CA beyond agreed duration is Additional Services

Schedule Extensions:
  [ALWAYS] Schedule extensions due to Owner delays do not constitute breach
  [ALWAYS] Extended duration entitles Architect to additional compensation

===============================================================================
RED FLAGS CHECKLIST (Reject or Escalate to Eran)
===============================================================================

[ ] Liability exceeds 2x total fees
[ ] Reimbursable cap below 10%
[ ] No consequential damages waiver
[ ] Payment terms exceeding 60 days
[ ] Cure period less than 15 days
[ ] No termination fee upon Owner convenience termination
[ ] Transfer of IP ownership (not just license)
[ ] Indemnification clauses that exceed standard of care
[ ] Requirement to guarantee construction cost or schedule
[ ] No defined Owner review/decision period
[ ] Arbitration outside of New York (for domestic projects)
