ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF SERVICES
===============================================================================

1.1 The Architect shall provide Design Architect services for the Project.
    RAK Associates shall serve as Executive Architect (Architect of Record)
    responsible for local code compliance, permit submissions, and
    construction administration in the UAE.

1.2 Design Architect Scope (ODA):
    a) Schematic Design (SD) - concept through approved SD
    b) Design Development (DD) - detailed design and specifications
    c) Construction Documents (CD) - design intent drawings
    d) Design Support during Construction - periodic review

1.3 Executive Architect Scope (RAK Associates - separate contract with Owner):
    a) Local code compliance review and adaptation
    b) Dubai Municipality permit submissions
    c) Construction Documents - local compliance and coordination
    d) Full-time Construction Administration

1.4 Coordination: The Design Architect and Executive Architect shall
    coordinate through bi-weekly design meetings and shared BIM models.
    The Design Architect shall have design approval authority over all
    exterior and key interior design elements.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 5: COMPENSATION
===============================================================================

5.1 Total Fee
    The Owner shall pay the Architect a total fee of Five Million Six
    Hundred Thousand US Dollars ($5,600,000) for Design Architect services.

5.2 Fee Method
    The fee is based on a Percentage of Construction Cost.
    Estimated Construction Cost: $125,000,000
    Fee Percentage: 4.48%
    
    If the actual construction cost varies by more than 15% from the
    estimate, the fee shall be adjusted proportionally.

5.3 Phase Distribution

    Phase                        Percentage    Amount
    Schematic Design             20%           $1,120,000
    Design Development           30%           $1,680,000
    Construction Documents       35%           $1,960,000
    Design Support (Construction) 15%          $840,000
    TOTAL                        100%          $5,600,000

5.4 Payment Terms
    Invoices shall be submitted monthly in US Dollars. Payment is due
    within 45 days of invoice receipt. Late payment interest: 1% per month.

5.5 Currency: All payments shall be made in US Dollars to the Architect's
    designated US bank account.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 7: REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
===============================================================================

7.1 Reimbursable expenses are in addition to the Basic Services fee and
    include:
    - International travel (business class airfare for principals, economy
      for staff, hotel, ground transportation, meals)
    - Printing and reproduction of documents
    - Physical models and presentation materials
    - Shipping of presentation boards and materials to Dubai
    - Visa fees and travel insurance
    - Local transportation in Dubai
    - Communication costs (international calls, video conferencing)

7.2 Reimbursable expenses shall not exceed 15% of the total fee
    ($840,000) without prior written approval from the Owner.

7.3 Travel expenses shall be reimbursed at the following guidelines:
    - Airfare: Business class for Principals, Premium Economy for staff
    - Hotel: Up to $450/night for Principals, $250/night for staff
    - Meals: Up to $150/day per person
    - Ground transportation: Actual cost

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 8: ARCHITECT'S INSURANCE
===============================================================================

8.1 The Architect shall maintain the following insurance coverage:

    a) Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions):
       $5,000,000 per occurrence / $10,000,000 aggregate

    b) General Liability:
       $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate

    c) Workers' Compensation:
       As required by applicable law

8.2 Professional Liability coverage shall be maintained for a period of
    5 years following Substantial Completion of the Project.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 9: LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
===============================================================================

9.1 The Architect's total liability to the Owner for all claims arising
    from this Agreement shall not exceed 1.5 times the total fees paid
    to the Architect under this Agreement.

9.2 For claims arising from willful misconduct or gross negligence, the
    Architect's liability shall not exceed 2 times the total fees paid.

9.3 The Architect shall not be liable for:
    a) Consequential, special, or punitive damages
    b) Claims arising from the Executive Architect's services
    c) Claims arising from construction means, methods, or safety
    d) Delays caused by Owner changes or force majeure events

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 11: TERMINATION
===============================================================================

11.1 Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this
     Agreement upon 60 days' written notice.

11.2 Upon termination, the Owner shall pay the Architect for:
     a) All services performed to the date of termination
     b) All reimbursable expenses incurred
     c) A termination fee equal to 10% of the remaining unearned fee
     d) Non-refundable travel expenses already booked

11.3 Termination for Cause: 20-day cure period for material breach.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 12: GOVERNING LAW
===============================================================================

12.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

12.2 Disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in New York City under
     the rules of the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC).
