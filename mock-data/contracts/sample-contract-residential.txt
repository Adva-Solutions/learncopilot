===============================================================================
OWNER-ARCHITECT AGREEMENT
FOR ARCHITECTURAL SERVICES
===============================================================================

PROJECT: Riverside Tower
         265,000 SF Residential Rental Building
         New York, NY

OWNER:   Meridian Development Group LLC
         450 Park Avenue, Suite 2200
         New York, NY 10022

ARCHITECT: ODA Architecture PLLC
           1 Hudson Yards, Suite 2501
           New York, NY 10001

DATE OF AGREEMENT: June 1, 2024

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF SERVICES
===============================================================================

1.1 The Architect shall provide the following Basic Services for the Project:
    a) Schematic Design (SD)
    b) Design Development (DD)
    c) Construction Documents (CD)
    d) Bidding and Negotiation
    e) Construction Administration (CA)

1.2 Schematic Design Phase
    The Architect shall prepare schematic design documents based on the
    Owner's program requirements, including:
    - Site analysis and zoning analysis
    - Preliminary floor plans, sections, and elevations
    - Building massing studies
    - Preliminary unit layouts for all unit types
    - Schematic sustainability strategy
    - Preliminary code analysis
    - Estimated construction cost

1.3 Design Development Phase
    The Architect shall prepare design development documents, including:
    - Refined floor plans with dimensions
    - Exterior facade design and material selections
    - Interior design concepts for common areas
    - Coordination with engineering consultants
    - Updated code analysis
    - Updated cost estimate

1.4 Construction Documents Phase
    The Architect shall prepare construction documents, including:
    - Complete architectural drawing set for permit submission
    - Specifications (Division 1 and architectural divisions)
    - Coordination with all consultants
    - Building Department submission and coordination
    - Responses to plan examiner comments

1.5 Bidding and Negotiation Phase
    The Architect shall assist the Owner in:
    - Preparing bidding documents
    - Conducting pre-bid conferences
    - Responding to bidder questions (addenda)
    - Evaluating bids and providing recommendations

1.6 Construction Administration Phase
    The Architect shall provide:
    - Review of shop drawings and submittals
    - Responses to RFIs (Requests for Information)
    - Periodic site visits (minimum 2 per month)
    - Review of contractor payment applications
    - Punch list preparation and final inspection

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 2: OWNER'S RESPONSIBILITIES
===============================================================================

2.1 The Owner shall provide the Architect with:
    - Program requirements and project budget
    - Geotechnical report and survey
    - Environmental assessments
    - Access to the site for investigation
    - Timely review and approval of design documents
    - Payment in accordance with Article 5

2.2 The Owner shall engage and pay for the following consultants directly:
    - Structural Engineer
    - MEP Engineer
    - Civil Engineer
    - Landscape Architect
    - Geotechnical Engineer

2.3 The Owner shall designate a representative authorized to make decisions
    on behalf of the Owner. Decisions shall be made within 14 calendar days
    of submission unless otherwise agreed.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 3: SCHEDULE
===============================================================================

3.1 The Architect shall perform services in accordance with the following
    schedule from Notice to Proceed:

    Phase                    Duration
    Schematic Design         10 weeks
    Design Development       14 weeks
    Construction Documents   20 weeks
    Permitting               8 weeks (concurrent with CD completion)
    Bidding & Negotiation    6 weeks
    Construction Admin       24 months (estimated construction duration)

3.2 The schedule assumes:
    - Owner review periods of 2 weeks at each phase completion
    - No significant scope changes after DD completion
    - Timely consultant deliverables
    - Standard Building Department review timeline

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 4: ADDITIONAL SERVICES
===============================================================================

4.1 The following services are not included in Basic Services and shall be
    provided as Additional Services upon written authorization:

    a) Interior design services beyond common areas
    b) Furniture, fixtures, and equipment (FF&E) procurement
    c) LEED or sustainability certification documentation
    d) Renderings and marketing materials beyond 3 views
    e) Model making
    f) Expert witness services
    g) Services necessitated by Owner-directed changes after DD approval
    h) Services resulting from code changes enacted after the Agreement date
    i) Extended Construction Administration beyond 24 months

4.2 Additional Services shall be compensated at the following hourly rates:
    - Principal: $285/hour
    - Project Architect: $195/hour
    - Senior Designer: $165/hour
    - Designer: $135/hour
    - BIM Technician: $115/hour

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 5: COMPENSATION
===============================================================================

5.1 Total Fee
    The Owner shall pay the Architect a total fee of Three Million One
    Hundred Thousand Dollars ($3,100,000) for Basic Services.

5.2 Fee Method
    The fee is based on Time and Resources, with the total fee amount
    serving as a not-to-exceed cap. The Architect shall notify the Owner
    if projected costs are likely to exceed 90% of the fee cap.

5.3 Phase Distribution
    The fee shall be distributed among phases as follows:

    Phase                    Percentage    Amount
    Schematic Design         15%           $465,000
    Design Development       25%           $775,000
    Construction Documents   30%           $930,000
    Bidding & Negotiation    10%           $310,000
    Construction Admin       20%           $620,000
    TOTAL                    100%          $3,100,000

5.4 Payment Terms
    Invoices shall be submitted monthly based on percentage of phase
    completion. Payment is due within 30 days of invoice receipt.
    Interest on overdue payments: 1.5% per month.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 6: INSTRUMENTS OF SERVICE
===============================================================================

6.1 All drawings, specifications, and other documents prepared by the
    Architect are Instruments of Service and remain the property of the
    Architect. The Owner is granted a license to use the Instruments of
    Service for the construction and operation of this Project only.

6.2 The license to use Instruments of Service is contingent upon full
    payment of all fees due to the Architect.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 7: REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
===============================================================================

7.1 Reimbursable expenses are in addition to the Basic Services fee and
    include:
    - Printing and reproduction of documents
    - Postage and delivery
    - Permit and filing fees paid by the Architect on behalf of the Owner
    - Travel expenses outside the New York metropolitan area
    - Renderings and physical models requested by the Owner
    - Consultant coordination expenses

7.2 Reimbursable expenses shall not exceed 10% of the total fee
    ($310,000) without prior written approval from the Owner.

7.3 Reimbursable expenses shall be billed at cost plus a 10% markup
    for administrative handling.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 8: ARCHITECT'S INSURANCE
===============================================================================

8.1 The Architect shall maintain the following insurance coverage:

    a) Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions):
       $3,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate

    b) General Liability:
       $2,000,000 per occurrence / $4,000,000 aggregate

    c) Workers' Compensation:
       As required by New York State law

    d) Automobile Liability:
       $1,000,000 combined single limit

8.2 The Architect shall provide certificates of insurance to the Owner
    within 10 days of Agreement execution and upon each renewal.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 9: LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
===============================================================================

9.1 The Architect's total liability to the Owner for all claims arising
    from this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid to the
    Architect under this Agreement.

9.2 The Architect shall not be liable for consequential, special, or
    punitive damages.

9.3 The Owner's exclusive remedy for any claim shall be the correction
    of deficient services by the Architect at no additional cost.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 10: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
===============================================================================

10.1 Disputes shall first be addressed through direct negotiation between
     the parties' principals for a period of 30 days.

10.2 If negotiation is unsuccessful, disputes shall be submitted to
     mediation in New York City.

10.3 If mediation is unsuccessful, disputes shall be resolved by binding
     arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

===============================================================================
ARTICLE 11: TERMINATION
===============================================================================

11.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days' written
     notice to the other party.

11.2 Upon termination, the Owner shall pay the Architect for:
     a) All services performed to the date of termination
     b) All reimbursable expenses incurred to the date of termination
     c) A termination fee equal to 5% of the remaining unearned fee

11.3 Termination for Cause: If either party materially breaches this
     Agreement, the non-breaching party may terminate upon 30 days'
     written notice, provided the breaching party fails to cure the
     breach within the 30-day notice period.
