ODA ARCHITECTURE - PROJECT CASE STUDY

WYNWOOD ARTS MIXED-USE
Miami, FL

OVERVIEW
The Wynwood Arts Mixed-Use development is a 295,000-square-foot mixed-use building in the heart of Miami's Wynwood Arts District. The project combines 180 residential rental units with 35,000 square feet of ground-floor retail and gallery space, creating a vibrant live-work-create environment that reflects the artistic energy of the neighborhood.

PROJECT DATA
Typology: Mixed-Use (Residential + Retail/Gallery)
Location: Wynwood Arts District, Miami, FL
Size: 295,000 gross SF
Units: 180 residential (studio, 1BR, 2BR)
Retail/Gallery: 35,000 SF
Floors: 16 above grade, 2 below grade (parking)
Parking: 220 spaces
Construction Cost: $61,000,000
Completion: 2025
Client: Wynwood Development Partners
Sustainability: LEED Platinum Certified

DESIGN NARRATIVE
ODA's design for the Wynwood Arts Mixed-Use building draws on the neighborhood's identity as Miami's creative epicenter. The building's most distinctive feature is its porous ground level, where the facade dissolves into a series of covered outdoor galleries and retail arcades that extend the street life of Wynwood into the building itself.

The residential tower above is organized around a central courtyard that brings natural light deep into the floor plate while creating a shared amenity space with lush tropical landscaping. The facade alternates between solid precast concrete panels and floor-to-ceiling glass, establishing a rhythmic pattern that plays with light and shadow throughout the day.

Sustainability was integral to the design from the outset. The project achieved LEED Platinum certification through a combination of high-performance building envelope design, a 42% reduction in energy consumption compared to the ASHRAE baseline, and the use of over 60% recycled content in the structural concrete. A green roof system on the amenity level reduces urban heat island effect while providing outdoor space for residents.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES
- Porous ground floor with covered galleries connecting to Wynwood streetscape
- Central courtyard with tropical landscaping and resident amenities
- High-performance facade with precast concrete and floor-to-ceiling glazing
- Rooftop amenity deck with pool, fitness center, and co-working lounge
- LEED Platinum certification with 42% energy reduction
- 35,000 SF of gallery and retail space supporting local artists and businesses
