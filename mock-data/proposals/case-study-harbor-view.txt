ODA ARCHITECTURE - PROJECT CASE STUDY

HARBOR VIEW RESIDENCES
Jersey City, NJ

OVERVIEW
Harbor View Residences is a 210,000-square-foot luxury condominium development on the Jersey City waterfront, offering panoramic views of the Manhattan skyline and New York Harbor. The 32-story tower contains 165 residences ranging from studios to four-bedroom penthouses, with a comprehensive amenity program designed to compete with Manhattan's most prestigious addresses at a more accessible price point.

PROJECT DATA
Typology: Residential Condominium
Location: Jersey City, NJ (Waterfront)
Size: 210,000 gross SF
Units: 165 residential
Floors: 32 above grade, 1 below grade
Parking: 85 spaces (podium)
Construction Cost: $44,000,000
Completion: 2025 (Construction Administration phase)
Client: Meridian Development Group
Sustainability: LEED Gold Certified

DESIGN NARRATIVE
The Harbor View Residences tower was conceived as a "living room on the waterfront" -- a building where every unit, every amenity, and every common space takes advantage of the site's extraordinary views. ODA's design maximizes waterfront exposure through a slender tower form that rotates slightly from the street grid, ensuring that every unit has at least one window oriented toward the harbor or the Manhattan skyline.

The building's base contains a two-story lobby with a dramatic double-height glass wall framing the Statue of Liberty. The residential amenity level on the fifth floor wraps around an outdoor terrace with an infinity-edge pool that appears to merge with the harbor beyond. Interior finishes throughout the building reference the maritime context through warm wood tones, natural stone, and bronze metalwork accents.

The tower's facade employs a curtain wall system with high-performance low-E glazing and integrated sunshades on the south and west elevations. The sunshade system reduces solar heat gain by 35% while preserving unobstructed views -- a critical design challenge that ODA resolved through extensive energy modeling and facade performance testing.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES
- Rotated tower form maximizing waterfront views from all units
- Double-height lobby with harbor-framing glass wall
- Fifth-floor amenity terrace with infinity pool and harbor views
- High-performance curtain wall with integrated sunshade system
- Maritime-inspired interior material palette
- Waterfront promenade connecting to Jersey City's Hudson River Walkway
