DESIGN PHILOSOPHY:
ODA's design philosophy centers on porosity -- creating buildings that blur the boundary between public and private space. We believe that great architecture serves both the individual and the community, and our designs consistently deliver innovative solutions that enhance urban life while exceeding market expectations.
