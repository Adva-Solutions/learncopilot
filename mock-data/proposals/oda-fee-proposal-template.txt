===============================================================================
PRICING METHOD SELECTION
===============================================================================

ODA uses three methods for calculating architectural fees. Select the most
appropriate method based on project characteristics:

METHOD 1: TIME & RESOURCES
Best for: Complex or unusual projects, renovations, projects with undefined
scope, projects where the client wants transparency on hours.

METHOD 2: PERCENTAGE OF CONSTRUCTION COST
Best for: New construction with a clear program, projects where construction
cost is well established, competitive situations where percentage comparison
is expected.

METHOD 3: FIXED FEE
Best for: Repeat project types where ODA has strong cost history, projects
with clearly defined scope, clients who prefer budget certainty.
