Workshop Arc — Per Department

Each department walks the same 8-lesson path, but with their own dataset, narrative, images, and final custom agents. Every lesson's output feeds the next. The arc culminates in Outlook — a peer-to-peer email to an ODA colleague who might be interested in applying AI to their own work, sharing what was produced without overstating. The journey ends with two custom agents: a Write Like Me (voice) and a department-specific agent (process).

The peer-to-peer email pattern

In every department, the Outlook lesson is the culminating Apps step. Copilot is used in an integrated, practical way to: (1) surface an ODA colleague who'd be interested in seeing AI applied to their own work practice, (2) reference the actual outputs produced in prior lessons (Page, Image, Word v2, Excel dashboard), (3) tell the story in a peer-to-peer tone without overstating — no claims about time saved, efficiency gains, or transformation, (4) close with an open invitation to compare notes, not a pitch. The student experiences their workshop progress as a concrete thing they can share with a colleague, in their own tentative, grounded voice.
